Deployment & Product Challenges

aeos365 Project Proposal

Structural Inflexibility

Deployment Rigidity

Vendors force a binary choice between SaaS-only or On-Premises-only. Organizations are trapped in a single model with no clear migration path as needs evolve.

Monolithic Bundling

Organizations are forced to purchase entire ERP suites even when needing only specific modules, leading to bloatware and paying for unused functionality.

Market & Financial Impact

High Implementation Costs

$100K - $5M+

Substantial upfront investment for licenses & infrastructure, often prohibitive for SMEs.

Extended Timelines

14 - 25 Months

Average duration for deployment delays business benefits and drains internal resources.

Vendor Lock-in Risk

Proprietary systems and data formats limit portability, making migration to modern platforms costly and technically difficult.
