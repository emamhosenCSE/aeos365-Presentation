Operational & UX Challenges

aeos365 Project Proposal

Operational Friction

Fragmented Point Solutions

Cross-team operations break when isolated tools require repeated data handoffs and reconciliation.

Limited Access Control

Coarse-grained permissions create a trade-off between security and day-to-day execution speed.

Lack of Intelligent Assistance

Users often depend on manuals or support teams because contextual guidance is limited inside workflows.

SME Accessibility Gap

SMEs outgrow simple tools quickly, but full enterprise suites remain difficult to adopt and operate.

Impact on Business Operations

Integration Overhead

A significant share of IT effort is spent maintaining integration pipelines between disconnected systems.

Productivity Loss

Inconsistent UI/UX across different applications increases training time and user error rates.

Security Gaps

Coarse-grained permissions often force admins to grant "all-or-nothing" access.

Growth Ceiling

SMEs hit a wall when basic tools can't scale, but enterprise ERPs remain unaffordable.

"The practical challenge is not only software capability, but operational continuity across teams, roles, and systems."
