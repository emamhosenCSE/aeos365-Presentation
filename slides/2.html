Abstract & Overview

aeos365 Project Proposal

The Challenge

  • Fragmented Tools: Enterprises struggle with multiple disjointed vendors.

  • Data Silos: Lack of integration creates substantial operational overhead.

  • High Costs: Traditional ERPs are too expensive for most SMEs.

  • Rigid Deployment: Forced choice between SaaS or On-Premises with no flexibility.

The Solution: aeos365

An innovative modular enterprise platform featuring a unique dual-deployment architecture.

Core Innovation

Dual-deployment supporting both Multi-Tenant SaaS and Standalone distribution from a single codebase.

Architecture

Monorepo with independent packages, 4-Level RBAC, and complete database isolation per tenant.

Functional Scope

HRM CRM ERP Finance Inventory POS Project Management Document Management Quality Compliance AI Assistant

Goal: Deliver a unified, cloud-native ecosystem with flexible distribution and consistent UX.
